FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse through our restaurant listings, select your favorite dishes, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout.

What is the delivery time?

Delivery times vary by restaurant, but most orders are delivered within 25-35 minutes.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Use the Order Tracking section above with your Order ID to track in real-time.

Is there a minimum order value?

Minimum order values vary by restaurant but typically range from ₹99 to ₹199.
